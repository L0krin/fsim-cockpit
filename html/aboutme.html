I am in my senior year working on my bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I like working with computers and small electronics, but I also plan to get my private pilot's license after graduation. So I figured I would combine all of the above and build myself a home cockpit to play flight simulator.

I'm a big supporter of Open-Source hardware and software, this website is hosted using CentOS, Docker CE, and Apache.
